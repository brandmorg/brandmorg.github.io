There And Back Again is a fan site created by Lord of the Rings obsessed-fanboy, Branden Morgan.

A United States Marine Corps veteran, Branden was born in Abilene, TX before moving to Las Vegas, NV when he was five years old. He grew up reading Lord of the Rings and other fantasy novels growing up, being exposed to things such as Dungeons & Dragons at a young age to help build his imagination.


To this day, every year he does a full read through of the trilogy - and has begun reading The Hobbit to his eldest daughter, Raven (pictured above), before bed time.

From Branden:


"My favorite version of the books is the 50th Anniversary Edition Deluxe set. I'm a huge fan of the 'all-in-one' style of books which make it easier to read the whole trilogy in one go versus having to move from book to book as you progress through the story."

From Branden:


"As a huge fan of Dungeons & Dragons, I'm also a fan of other roleplaying games such as Dungeon Crawl Classics and MORK BORG. However, a company known as Free League produced a tabletop RPG based off of Tolkien's work called "The One Ring Roleplaying Game" - which I am currently a HUGE fan of - as it allows to mix two of my favorite things ever."
